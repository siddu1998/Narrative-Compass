About Narrative Compass

Narrative Compass is a game studio dedicated to transforming stories and experiences into engaging, playable products. We believe that the best way to connect with stories is through interactive play—where players become active participants in the narratives we create.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between storytelling and gameplay, crafting products that don't just tell stories, but let you live them. Whether it's through card games, board games, or digital experiences, we design every product with the goal of bringing stories directly into your hands.

Tales of Dharma is our flagship strategy card game, where ancient Indian mythology comes alive through strategic gameplay. But it's just the beginning—we're constantly exploring new ways to deliver stories, products, and experiences that resonate with players of all ages.

At Narrative Compass, play isn't just entertainment—it's how we share stories, build connections, and create memorable experiences that last long after the game ends.
