Frequently Asked Questions

Does my general count towards the diverse sena victory?
Yes, the class of your general counts towards having a character from each of the six classes.
Can I attack asuras with my general?
No, you cannot attack asuras with your general. Only Majors can be nominated to attack asuras.
Can anyone play shakuni cards at any point?
Yes! Any player can play the Shakuni card to support or sabotage any player. Remember a Shakuni card manipulates only the latest dice roll.
Do I need to attempt and invoke the major as soon as I introduce a major into my sena?
Yes, you need to attempt and invoke the major by rolling your dice when you introduce a major into your sena.
Can I clash the invoking of a major?
No, Clash cards can only be used against the introduction of a major into the sena, playing a maya card, attaching astras and curses.
